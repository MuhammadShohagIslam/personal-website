Our Work for Children in Bangladesh

Since 1970, Save the Children has worked with children, families, communities, and government departments in Bangladesh, including sponsorship since 1972.

With a large in-country team and hundreds of partners, we are one of the major child charities in Bangladesh, offering a wide range of programs, including those supporting children's basic rights to good health.
